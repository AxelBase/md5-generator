Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: November 23, 2025

At AxelBase MD5 Hash Generator, your privacy is not just respected — it is guaranteed by design. This tool operates 100% client-side in your browser. That means:

  • No data is ever transmitted to any server
  • No personal information is collected, stored, or processed
  • No cookies, analytics, or tracking pixels are used
  • No logs, IP addresses, or usage data are recorded

How It Works

When you paste text or upload a file:

  • All MD5 computation happens locally using JavaScript (spark-md5)
  • File reading uses the browser’s native File API
  • Large files are processed incrementally in memory chunks
  • Copy-to-clipboard uses the secure Clipboard API

Your data never leaves your device. Even if you close the tab, nothing is retained.

Third-Party Services

We do not use any external analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The site is statically hosted on GitHub Pages with no backend. Open-source libraries (Bootstrap, spark-md5) are loaded from trusted CDNs but do not track users.

Cookies & Local Storage

No cookies are set. The only possible local storage usage is temporary (e.g., your input during a session) and is cleared automatically when you refresh or close the browser.

Children’s Privacy

This tool is not directed at children under 13. Since we collect no data, we do not knowingly process children’s information.

International Compliance

Because we collect zero personal data, we are compliant by default with GDPR, CCPA, LGPD, and all major privacy regulations worldwide.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy if new features are added. Changes will be posted here with the updated effective date.

Contact

Questions? Open an issue on our GitHub repository.

You are in full control. Your files, your hashes, your privacy — always.
